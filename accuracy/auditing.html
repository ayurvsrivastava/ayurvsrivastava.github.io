Audit Services ·

Audited Financial Services

An audit is the highest level of assurance any certified accountant can provide, so we pride ourselves in giving the highest and best work. To work as an auditor, an in-depth understanding of the credentials. All audit services are performed in accordance with auditing standards generally accepted in the United States. The goal of an audit is the distribution of an independent audit report giving reasonable assurance that the financial statements are free of material misstatements.

401k Retirement Plan Audits

The audit of a retirement plan requires specialized knowledge and attention. Companies are focusing on finding a provider that offers the highest level of quality and care of this process. Accuracy Accounting focuses on providing retirement plan audits to our clients. We work with emerging single employer 401k plans, with 125 participants to established multi-employer plans, and with thousands of participants.

Reviewed Financial Statements

A review is less intensive than an audit. Analytic tools and inquiries of management are the primary activities performed, along with obtaining an understanding of the entity and the components of the financial statements. The goal of a review is the issuance of an accountants report providing limited assurance that there are no material misstatements in the financial statements.
